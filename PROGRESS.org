* 2026-06-19 bridge-005 stabilize YouTube bitrate and frame cadence

  `vbv_maxrate`; the target was not being ignored by the running bridge image.
- The browser compositor was driven only by `requestAnimationFrame`. Chromium throttles that

  data, leaving the transport less constant than YouTube's ingest contract expects.
